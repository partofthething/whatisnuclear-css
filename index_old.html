What is nuclear?

Learn about nuclear energy

A good choice to sustain the environment into the 22nd century

Every day, we're becoming more concerned about energy. Global warming, dependence on foreign oil, shortage of resources, and increasing population constantly top the headlines. Peaceful nuclear energy offers compelling solutions to many of these problems, but its public image is distorted at best. Here, we offer facts on both the good and bad aspects of nuclear energy, hoping to open up minds to this vast energy resource that is often disregarded.

Did you know

Standard nuclear waste has more than 90% of the uranium that was put in the reactor. There is enough recoverable energy in the waste accumulated today in the US to power the country for over 90 years.

Why don't we use this resource? The recycling of fuel was shut down in the '70s due mostly to proliferation concerns.

Today, new technology greatly reduces these concerns, but it remains prohibitively costly. Should we bury this resource or start recycling? Find out more.
